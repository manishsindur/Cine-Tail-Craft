# Cine Tail Craft

A comprehensive platform to explore and track your favorite movies, series, and anime. Get detailed information on where to watch, user ratings, and create your personalized watch list with our bookmark feature.

## Key Features :-

- Explore Content: Browse through an extensive library of movies, series, and anime.
- Watch Availability: Discover where your favorite content is streaming.
- Ratings & Reviews: Check out ratings to help you decide what to watch next.
- Personalized Watch List: Bookmark and organize titles for future viewing.
